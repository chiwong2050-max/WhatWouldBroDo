Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

Quick summary

  • This app is a parody/entertainment experience.
  • Community Struggles are stored only on your device (localStorage) unless you add a backend.
  • We do not need your real name, email, or phone number to function.

Data we store on your device

If you use the “Submit Your Struggle” feature, the text you submit is saved locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This is used only to show your last 5 community struggles on this same device.

You can clear this data by clearing your site data in your browser settings (or uninstalling the PWA and clearing data).

Analytics and ads

This project includes placeholder scripts for Google Analytics and AdSense that are intended to be replaced with your own IDs. If you enable analytics/ads in production, those providers may collect usage data and set cookies according to their policies.

Recommended: add a consent banner and only load analytics/ads after consent where required by law.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your local submissions because they are not transmitted anywhere by this app.
  • We do not ask you for passwords or payment details.

Contact
